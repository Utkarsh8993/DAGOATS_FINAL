F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can use the website?

    The website is free to use for IIT Roorkee students.

  • What is the Voting System like?

    The Voting system is like a general Ballot system.

  • How many points for each preference while voting?

    The First preference is awarded 5 points while the Second preference is awarded with 3 points with the Third preference candidate being given 1 point.

  • How do I know who won?

    After the voting information will be displayed on the website on when the results will be published.

  • Can I add an event for other students?

    Yes, you can add events for viewing by other students.

  • Is there any additional features of IITR Events?

    IITR Events also allows users to select particular events they want to attend and they will be notified when the particular event is taking place.
